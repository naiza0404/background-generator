Biography

Naiza Dusoruth (Born April 4,1997) is a walking meme factory, a procrastinator pundit, a people pleaser prodigy and last but not least a gastronomy guru. This tribute page will be dedicated to my younger self who was full of doubt and fear, who thought her dreams were out of reach and that success were meant for each but kept going and growing and found life overflowing.

You know that one ice breaker question that goes if you could talk to any person dead or alive, who would it be? I would pick my younger self. I would hug her, tell her am sorry for what's to come. She was so sweet, so kind, she does not deserve the way i treated her.
